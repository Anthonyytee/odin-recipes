Tacos Al Pastor

Description

Pastor is tender pork, marinated in a delicious sauce. Then it is slowly grilled, thinly sliced, and served with fresh pineapple, cilantro, and chopped onions.

Ingredients
  • 2-3 pound Pork Loin cut into thin slices
  • Guajillo Chile boiled, de-seeded, and cleaned.
  • 1 Ancho Chile boiled, de-seeded, and cleaned.
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 3/4 cup of pineapple juice fresh is better
  • 1 Tablespoon of Organo
  • 1 Tablespoon of Thyme
  • 1 Tablespoon of Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of Cumin
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of Paprika
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of Salt
  • 3 Cloves
Steps
  1. Blend all of the ingredients together until completely combined.
  2. Strain the liquid over a bowl to end up with a smooth marinade.
  3. Add one layer of meat in a large bowl or baking dish and cover with the marinade, and then repeat in layers
  4. until all of the meat and marinade have been used.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best)
  6. Remove from the refrigerator and grill the meat until cooked through.
  7. Remove from grill and cut into thin slices to serve on the tacos.
